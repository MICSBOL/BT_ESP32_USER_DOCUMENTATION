Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This policy describes how MICSBOL TeleCon4ESP32 (“the app”, package com.micsbol.emitterapp) handles information when you use it to control RC hardware over Bluetooth.

1. Who we are

The app is published by MICSBOL. For privacy questions, contact us at the email shown on the About screen in the app.

2. Information the app processes

  • Bluetooth: The app scans for, pairs with, and connects to Bluetooth devices you choose (for example an ESP32). It sends control commands and receives telemetry from your hardware. This data stays on your phone and is transmitted only to the device you connect to. We do not operate a cloud server that receives your RC or Bluetooth traffic.
  • Device and connection metadata: To reconnect quickly, the app may store the name and address of the last Bluetooth device you connected to, and your connection status, on your device only.
  • App settings: Joystick behavior, switch defaults, knob defaults, and related preferences are stored locally on your device (DataStore).
  • Documents: PDF guides and ZIP firmware files bundled in the app are read or copied locally when you open or export them. We do not upload those files to our servers.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The app displays banner ads through Google AdMob. Google may collect identifiers and usage data to serve and measure ads, as described in the Google Privacy Policy and How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services. You can limit ad personalization in your Android settings (Google → Ads).

4. Permissions

The app requests Bluetooth permissions to discover and connect to your controller. It does not require location for Bluetooth scanning (neverForLocation). Vibration may be used for feedback where supported by the device.

5. What we do not do

  • We do not require an account or collect your name, email, or phone number inside the app.
  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not upload your Bluetooth control or telemetry streams to MICSBOL servers.

6. Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Security and retention

Locally stored preferences remain on your device until you clear app data or uninstall the app. Bluetooth pairing may also be managed in your system Bluetooth settings.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

9. Your rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data. Because most data is local or handled by Google for ads, you can clear app storage, adjust ad settings, or contact us for other requests.
